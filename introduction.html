Introduction

Who wrote this? Are they even qualified? What do they want?

Who wrote this?

This was written by The Creator, a curious English major attempting to break into the realm of programming and website design. Though he is more oriented around topics of business and literature, he is developing a slow fascination for technology and data analytics. He hopes to one day go to law school, and work in the legal fields involved most with innovative technologies. Apparently “space law” is a developing field, maybe he will go for that.

Are they even qualified?

As a beginner “programmer,” The Creator is probably not entirely qualified to build official websites. He is however, training under the tutelage of a great professor. Besides ENGL 3844, he has had no real training in digital writing. He once ran a badly constructed tumblr for a couple weeks. Perhaps with some practice, he will be able to better assemble a portfolio of well-constructed websites and designs. After Writing and Digital Media, he has big dreams to move on to the art of programming in java. As pictured to the left, The Creator is a VT shaped student and experienced in doing other things, including examining oversized cheeses in Amsterdam.

What does he want?

Technology seems to be at the forefront of all fruitful careers, and The Creator has taken notice. English degrees provide some powerfully useful skills, but there are whole other worlds of knowledge to absorb beyond English, and he is intent on understanding these fields too. Technology now serves as the foundation of countless many companies. Thorough understanding of technology in business is sure to only lead to good things. Hopefully, he will be able to apply these skills to business, and leverage himself into a career of international travel. Here he is in Zurich, Switzerland.
