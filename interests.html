What does The Creator do at VT?

The Creator is a student at Virginia Tech interested working in the business of innovative technologies. He hopes to one day go to law school, and plans on using lessons learned from Virginia Tech as a spring board into an optimistic future. He is sure to apply this recent coding education to business objectives, and intends to enter the fields of consulting and finance.

Digital Writing

Though still a beginner at the art of digital writing, The Creator does believe that with hard work and determination, he will be able to skillfully apply lessons learned in class to a possible future with digital media. His interests are ingrained in technological advancement, so it is likely that programming skills will come in handy. Besides the noted professional intersect, he also just finds programming to be interesting, and fun. Here he is Berlin, Germany carrying out his agenda as a VT shaped student.

The VT shaped student as a future

The work of the VT shaped student is one that signifies a clear combination of academically acquired knowledge, and practically learned skills. An example could be evidence that a student learns a certain technique in class, applies it to a real-world scenario, and gains practical knowledge from the entire process. In digital writing, an example of this could be found in the instance when a student learns an html component, practices it, and applies it to a medium in which they are personally interested in. For the future, The Creator is interested in working as a strategic consultant, a career which fundamentally embodies the VT shaped student by promoting classroom education, practical education, experiential learning, and well rounded skill sets. Here he is in Venice, exploring the food markets.
